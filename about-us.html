Founders Message

ConCap is not just a financial services firm, it’s a way of life.

Those who decide to become our clients are making a solemn commitment to diligently working towards their dreams and turning them into reality.

This is, admittedly, very difficult to do.  But it is possible with a solid financial plan, with tremendous diligence, with pain-staking patience, with steady discipline, and an unflinching determination and resolve, and your financial advisor to guide you on your way, to turn those dreams into reality.

Most people cannot do it The ConCap Way, but some of you can. 

 And it is for you who can, that I created this company.  I want to invite those who are up to the challenge to come and join me.

Join me in living fearless!

VJ Arjan - Founder and CEO

VJ Arjan, CFP®

CEO of ConCap

At 19, I became fascinated with the power of investing and the idea of freeing myself from a paycheck-to-paycheck way of life. I subsequently attained my financial freedom in my late 20s and have been free of a full-time job ever since.

I now spend my life living fearlessly and helping others to live theirs The ConCap Way.
